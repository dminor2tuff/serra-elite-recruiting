Transcript / Records Request

College coaches and admissions staff may use this page to request official academic records for Serra Football student-athletes.

To request records:

  1. Click the button below to open an email pre-addressed to our staff.
  2. Complete the fields in the body of the email.
  3. Send from your institutional email address when possible.

Information to include:

  • Player full name
  • Graduation year
  • Date of birth
  • Parent / guardian name
  • Contact phone and email
  • College / university requesting records
  • Type of records needed (official transcript, unofficial, progress report, etc.)

Email Records Request to serracavsfb@gmail.com
