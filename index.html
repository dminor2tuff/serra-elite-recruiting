Why Serra Football

Junípero Serra High School is nationally recognized for developing elite football talent within a culture of discipline, toughness, accountability, and academic excellence. Our student-athletes are prepared for success at the collegiate level and beyond.

What We Expect From Our Athletes

Serra Football athletes are expected to lead in the classroom, compete relentlessly, respect the game, and represent the program with pride. Accountability, coachability, work ethic, and character are non-negotiables.
