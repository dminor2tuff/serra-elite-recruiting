Why Serra Football

Proven Competition

Serra competes at the highest levels of California high school football, facing top-ranked programs and preparing athletes for the speed and physicality of college ball.

Development & Detail

We emphasize fundamentals, film study, and accountability. Serra athletes learn how to practice like pros and prepare like pros.

Recruiting Structure

Our staff organizes film, transcripts, testing data, and verified measurables to give college coaches clear, organized information on each prospect.

What We Expect from Our Athletes

Academics First

Be on track to qualify. Go to class, communicate with teachers, and compete in the classroom the same way you compete on the field.

Character & Accountability

Be coachable, be on time, and do things the right way when nobody is watching. Your name, your family, and Serra Football are on everything you do.

Work Behind the Scenes

Weight room, speed work, recovery, and film. We expect our athletes to invest in their craft year-round and embrace the grind.

Serra Football has produced multiple NFL Draft picks, professional players, and All-American student-athletes. Our alumni have gone on to impact Power 5, Group of 5, FCS, Division II, Division III, NAIA, and JUCO programs across the country.

We take pride in developing complete young men: students, leaders, teammates, and competitors who bring value to every locker room they join.
