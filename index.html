Why Serra Football

Proven Competition

Serra competes at the highest levels of California high school football, preparing athletes for the speed and physicality of college football.

Structure & Detail

We emphasize fundamentals, film study, and professional habits. Our players learn how to prepare and practice like college athletes.

College Coach Ready

Organized profiles with film, academics, and background in one place — built to help college staffs evaluate quickly and efficiently.

What We Expect from Our Athletes

Academics First

Compete in the classroom. Be on track to qualify, communicate with teachers, and respect the academic side of being a student-athlete.

Character & Accountability

Be coachable, on time, and dependable. How you carry yourself reflects your family, Serra Football, and the locker rooms you will one day join.

Work Behind the Scenes

Embrace the weight room, speed work, recovery, and film. True development happens away from the cameras.

Serra Football Legacy

Serra Football has produced multiple NFL Draft picks, professional players, and All-American student-athletes. Our alumni have gone on to compete at the Power 5, Group of 5, FCS, Division II, Division III, NAIA, and JUCO levels.

We take pride in developing complete young men: students, leaders, teammates, and competitors who bring value to every program they join.
